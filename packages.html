Pricing built to start simple and scale with you.

A three-part model for small-business teams of roughly 3–75 people: a fixed-price starter bundle to get launched, the per-user Lifeline for ongoing managed support, and à la carte build projects whenever you need them.

Step 1 · Starter Bundles

Get launched with one fixed price.

Fixed price, fixed scope — the entry point for a new client. Each bundle pairs onboarding, an initial build, and a hypercare window at roughly 20–25% below à la carte, then transitions to its matching Lifeline tier.

Step 2 · The Lifeline

Ongoing support, billed per user.

Recurring managed support, billed per user per month on a 12-month minimum term. It scales automatically as your headcount changes — new seats are added at the tier rate, never re-quoted.
